Short Story

Adiva Graphics formally started in 2010. The dream behind Adiva Graphics was to address a major gap in the Market Research Industry. This bottleneck refers to Charting. While working at Millward Brown, Tamzid Alam Ziad noticed that even after completing surveys, collecting data and compiling them, the final product would be delayed due to a lack of efficiency/staffing/support from the charting department. Adiva Graphics is one of the few organizations in the world which cater exclusively to Charting.

The company is built on 35 years of Market Research experience. It caters to client in the United States. The company, since inception, has experienced rapid growth in terms of clients, project experience, and personnel. It is also led by 30+ years of sound business and HR expertise.

Our main business focus is charting. Additionally, we have integrated Survey Quality Checking / Data Tab Assurance, Debranding and Ad Transcripts to the business model. However, since Charting is our proverbial "Golden Goose", everyone is required, regardless of specific department, to expect that they will be charting 100% of their time here at Adiva Graphics.
